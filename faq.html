Frequently Asked Questions

What is passEE?

passEE is a comprehensive study companion app designed specifically for pharmacy professionals preparing for the PEBC Evaluating Exam. It offers over 2,900 practice questions, AI-powered assistance, and various study modes to optimize your exam preparation.

How do I purchase a subscription?

Subscriptions can be purchased through either the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Simply download the app on your preferred device and select your subscription plan (3 or 6 months) within the app. All payments are processed securely through Google Play or Apple's payment systems.

Can I use passEE on multiple devices?

Yes, you can use passEE on multiple devices with the same account. For Apple devices, use the same Apple ID. For Android devices, use the same Google account. However, to prevent account sharing, we implement a device switching cooldown system. Please refer to our Device Switching Policy for details.

Is my progress saved across devices?

Yes, all your progress, bookmarks, and study data are synchronized across your devices when you're signed in with the same account.

How often is the content updated?

We regularly update our question bank and content to ensure accuracy and relevance. Updates are automatically delivered through the Google Play Store and App Store.

What happens when my subscription expires?

When your subscription expires, you'll maintain access to your progress data but won't be able to access practice questions or study materials until you renew your subscription.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. For Android users, cancel through your Google Play Store settings. For Apple users, cancel through your Apple ID settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How does the AI assistance work?

Our AI system analyzes your performance and study patterns to provide personalized explanations, identify knowledge gaps, and suggest focused study areas. It also helps generate detailed answer rationales and summaries.

How has the PEBC Evaluating Exam changed recently?

As of October 2024, the PEBC Evaluating Exam has undergone several changes:

  • The number of questions has been reduced from 200 to 150
  • The exam duration is now approximately 3.25 hours
  • The exam consists of two sections with 75 questions each (90 minutes per section)
  • The exam is now offered four times per year instead of twice
  • The exam blueprint has been updated to reflect the new curriculum (effective June 2025)
