Understanding the passEE Brand Name

Our brand name is passEE (pronounced "pass-EE", with lowercase p and capital EE). We understand that people may search for us using various spellings and formats. This page explains all the different ways our brand name can be written and searched. All of these variations—passEE, passee, pass-ee, pass-EE, pass ee, and pass EE—refer to the same brand and will help you find our PEBC Evaluating Exam preparation platform.

All Brand Name Variations

The following are all valid ways to refer to and search for our brand. All of these variations are equivalent and refer to the same passEE platform:

  • passEE - Our official brand name (lowercase p, capital EE)
  • passee - All lowercase version
  • pass-ee - With hyphen, all lowercase (matches our domain: pass-ee.ca)
  • pass-EE - With hyphen, capital EE
  • pass ee - Two words, all lowercase
  • pass EE - Two words, capital EE

How to Search for passEE

When searching for our platform on Google or other search engines, you can use any of the variations listed above. All of these search terms will help you find our PEBC Evaluating Exam preparation platform:

  • Search for "passEE" to find our platform
  • Search for "passee" to find our platform
  • Search for "pass-ee" to find our platform
  • Search for "pass EE" to find our platform
  • Search for "passEE PEBC" for more specific results
  • Search for "passEE Evaluating Exam" for exam-specific content

Why So Many Variations?

Our official brand name is passEE, but we recognize that:

  • Our domain name is pass-ee.ca (with a hyphen), which may cause people to search with hyphens
  • Search engines may interpret "passEE" as two separate words: "pass" and "EE"
  • People naturally type brand names in different ways (with or without spaces, with or without hyphens, with different capitalization)
  • We want to ensure that no matter how someone searches for us, they can find our platform

Our Official Branding

While all variations are valid for searching, our official brand name used in marketing materials, social media, and official communications is:
