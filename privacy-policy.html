passEE Privacy Policy for PEBC Evaluating Exam Preparation Platform

Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Last Updated: January 1, 2026.
passEE is not affiliated with the Pharmacy Examining Board of Canada. "PEBC" and "Pharmacy Examining Board of Canada" are trademarks of the Pharmacy Examining Board of Canada.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

A. Personal Information:

Information provided during registration, such as your name, email address, and username.

B. Device Information:

Technical data such as your device type, operating system, and device or other IDs.

C. Messages:

Emails you send to us or receive from us.

D. Usage Data:

Information about how you interact with the app, including feature usage and preferences.

D. Payment Data:

Information required for in-app purchases, such as payment methods, is processed securely by trusted third-party payment providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Account Management: Secure login and personalized user experiences
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing app performance and enhancing features
  • Payment Processing: Managing subscriptions and purchases
  • Communication: Sending essential updates, notifications, and promotional materials (with your consent)

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties, companies, or organizations. Your information is only used within the app for the purposes described in this policy. We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your information. However, please note that some data may not be encrypted during transmission, and we cannot guarantee complete security. While we strive for maximum security, no system is completely immune to breaches.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights such as:

  • Accessing and updating your personal data
  • Requesting deletion of your data
  • Restricting certain processing activities

For requests, email us at support@npd-pass.com.

6. Updates to this Policy

We may revise this policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or app functionality. Material changes will be communicated through the app or email.
