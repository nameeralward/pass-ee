Take Your Next Step with passMCQ

Now that you've conquered the Evaluating Exam, it's time to prepare for the more challenging PEBC Qualifying Examination. The passMCQ app is specifically designed to help international pharmacy graduates succeed in this critical next step.

Built for Success

passMCQ builds on everything you loved about passEE, with advanced features specifically designed for the more complex Qualifying Examination:

  • Comprehensive Coverage: All therapeutic areas and competencies tested on the exam
  • Clinical Focus: Patient cases and scenarios that reflect real pharmacy practice
  • Adaptive Learning: The app focuses on your weak areas to maximize study efficiency
  • Exam Simulation: Practice under timed conditions that mirror the actual exam
