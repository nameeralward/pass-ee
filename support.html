Support

Contact Us

We're here to help! For any questions about PEBC Evaluating Exam preparation, subscription plans, or technical support, please email us at:

support@npd-pass.com

Support is available Monday to Friday (excluding holidays). We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.

Response Times (Business Days Only)

  • Technical Issues: 24-48 hours
  • Account Related: 24 hours
  • Billing Inquiries: 24-48 hours
  • General Questions: 48 hours

Before Contacting Support

To help us assist you better, please include:

  • Your account email address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Clear description of the issue
  • Screenshots (if applicable)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
